About Us

The English Nomad Theatre is a mobile English-language theatre company, bringing high-quality, accessible productions to towns, villages, festivals, and cultural events.

Our work is designed to travel: small casts, minimal technical needs, and performances that adapt easily to outdoor squares, community halls, and temporary stages.

Our Mission

To make English-language theatre available beyond major cities, connecting English-speaking communities with live performance wherever they are.
